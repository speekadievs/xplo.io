About

About

This is the most explosive game. Blow up other players and survive as long as possible

How to Play

Use W to drop a mine

Use SPACE to throw a grenade

Don't step on other player and YOUR own mines.

Strategy

The main goal of the game, is to blow up as many enemies as you possibly can. Use grenades when you are chasing someone and use mines when someone is chasing you. Remember to not step on other player and YOUR own mines.
